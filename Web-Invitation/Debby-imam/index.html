WHEN TWO SAME
ENERGIES MEET

Over the mountain and through the woods, Merbabu brought us to meet each other on 3.145 M.dpl hike and it will be ended to the wedding aisle.

When we first date, we talked a lot about hard times, dreams and goals. There’s a unique comfort in finding someone who has same energy.

We’re both know we have two different background. a Chinese-graduated railway engineer, meets a woman who hangs around in construction projects.

It's not easy to embrace each other when we're starting our careers in a new place. complementing, encouraging and reminding each other, become a fun thing to talk about in the midst of our endless busyness

This journey has made us grow. Of course, there was a fight, ego and tears. But, it makes our relationships become a place of mutual respect, understanding, and unspoken love

In the end, let us share not only stories and happiness, but also precious moments in our lives
